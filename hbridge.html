H-bridge

Project Description

I designed an H-bridge circuit on KiCad. Since this circuit can make a DC motor spin in both directions, it can be used to change the direction of a wheel. When my group and I built our robot later that year, we used two H-bridge circuits for each of the two wheels that we needed to power.
